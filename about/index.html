About Torspark

Torspark is a software consulting company run by me, Chris Olds. Yep, I'm a one man shop, and I'm proud of it. I don't see any reason to pretend that my company is any bigger. Besides, this means that you're always communicating with the person who will actually be doing the work. No need to worry about things getting "lost in translation" when the sales guy passes the work off to another employee.

I've been a software developer/consultant in one form or another since 2005. If you want to stretch the definition a little, I helped some companies develop their e-commerce solutions back in 2001. Torspark llc was officially created in early 2011.
