Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I make my purchase?

After you complete your purchase, we'll send you a brief questionnaire to fill out. This questionnaire will introduce us to your business, your site, your visitors, your clients, and scheduling options for those choosing a live video consultation.

What if I want you to review a page that is not launched yet?

In our online questionnaire we ask you to list the links of pages you would prefer us to review. (Or we review up to 3 of your top performing pages based on analytics.) If you would like us to review a page you have not launched yet, then just give us the access information in the questionnaire to view that page.

Why can’t I schedule the video consultation in less than 1 week?

We currently have multiple clients and projects that we are working on. 1 week guarantees that we have time to review your website in advance of your video consultation. This ensures you receive the highest quality review and information tailored to optimizing your website.
