Torspark Workshops

Torspark’s Lunch & Learn will teach you some of the best practices you need to know to improve your conversion rate.

Torspark's Small Group Workshop gives you an opportunity to get a more in-depth look at your specific website/landing page through practical application.

Lunch & Learn

Listen to Torspark’s CRO experts and learn how to improve your website and increase your conversions.

What to expect during the Lunch & Learn:
  • We'll give a 40 minute presentation on Conversion Optimization Best Practices while you enjoy lunch.
  • We'll use real example landing pages provided by attendees to highlight each point. Be sure to submit yours after you sign up!
  • After the presentation, we'll spend about 15 minutes answering any questions about everything we just covered.
What happens after you sign up for the Lunch & Learn:
  • You'll be asked to fill out a brief form to choose your lunch option & include your landing page in the presentation.
  • We'll take a look at your landing page and integrate it into our presentation.
  • You'll come to the Lunch & Learn, enjoy a tasty lunch, and learn a thing or two about improving conversions for your website.

* Improve Your Website Guarantee

If you submit your website and/or landing page, we'll use it in our presentation and give you at least one piece of actionable advice on how to improve your page for better conversions.

You'll get something specific about your page, plus insight from everyone else's landing pages, too!

Join our Lunch & Learn

Small Group Workshop (+ lunch)

Work with Torspark’s CRO experts to improve your website and increase your conversions, while also helping other attendees do the same.

What to expect during the Small Group Workshop:
  • We'll give a brief presentation on Conversion Optimization Best Practices while you enjoy the provided lunch.
  • After the presentation, we'll dedicate at least 10 minutes working on each attendee's landing page(s).
  • This is meant to be an interactive workshop where you'll walk away knowing how to improve your own pages. We'll all work together to brainstorm new ways to improve your website with the underlying goal of increasing your conversions.
What happens after you sign up for the Small Group Workshop:
  • You'll be asked to fill out a brief form to choose your lunch option & submit which landing page you'd like to improve.
  • We'll analyze your landing page and take some notes to guide our discussion during the workshop.
  • You'll come to the Small Group Workshop, enjoy a tasty lunch, and walk away with at least 7 ways to improve your landing page for conversions.

* Improve Your Website Guarantee × 7

When you submit your website and/or landing page, we'll make sure to spend enough time on it to ensure you get at least seven pieces of actionable advice on how to improve your page for better conversions.

You'll get plenty of specifics about your page, plus insight from everyone else's landing pages, too!
